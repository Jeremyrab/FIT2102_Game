Space Invaders

Controls

Left Arrow: Move Left
Right Arrow: Move Right
Space: Shoot
R : Reset

Point Shop (Press these buttons to purchase the following effect at the cost of your points)

Z: Regenerate Shields (5 Points)
X: Extra Life (10 Points)
A: Shoot Piercing Bullet Next (15 Points)
S: Shoot Wide Bullet Next (15 Points)
